Lasagna

This lasagna recipe is sure to blow everyone, and their mom's, minds. With its delicious riccotta and and perfectly portioned meat-sauce ratio you can not go wrong with this classic Italian dish.

    1 pound sweet Italian sausage
    .75 pound lean ground beef
    .5 cup minced onion
    2 cloves garlic, crushed
    1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
    2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
    2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
    .5 cup water
    2 tablespoons white sugar
    1.5 teaspoons dried basil leaves
    .5 teaspoon fennel seeds
    1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
    1.5 teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
    .25 teaspoon ground black pepper
    4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
    12 lasagna noodles
    16 ounces ricotta cheese
    1 egg
    .75 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
    .75 cup grated Parmesan cheese
